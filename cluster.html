Benilde Hive

Benilde Hive (BHive) is the first accredited LGBTQIA+ student organization in De La Salle Philippines. Founded in De La Salle-College of Saint Benilde, the organization aims to inform and educate, change perspectives, advocate for equality, and fight against the misconceptions and discrimination against the LGBTQIA+ community. We strive to create safe spaces, raise awareness of human rights, and promote inclusivity, first and foremost in Benilde, as well as the Philippine society.

Benilde Commission on Election

The Benilde Commission on Elections (COMELEC) is the sole, legitimate body tasked to hold, supervise, and regulate student elections of De La Salle - College of Saint Benilde, in accordance to the rules and regulations set by the Center for Student Life-Student Involvement Unit. The Commission is also responsible for the accreditation of political parties and approval of student coalitions within the college.
