Student Involvement Week is the annual recruitment, activity, and promotional event
for all accredited organizations within De La Salle-College of Saint Benilde. During the week, the accredited and recognized student organizations (ARSOs) are given the opportunity to show the activities and services that they can offer to the Benildean Community. Throughout the week, students may sign up for the organizations of their choice.

Drawing from the spirit of rebuilding, this Student Involvement Week seeks to reintroduce the Accredited and Recognized Student Organizations to the Benildean community as the school returns face-to-face classes. The theme of this SI Week is inspired by the look of magazines as each ARSO shall show uniqueness, diversity, and variety by following the prompt; “if your organization was a magazine, what magazine would it be?”. Additionally, the title “REPUBLISH” comes from the world of publication, defined as the act of publishing a piece of text again or issuing a new edition; this coincides with the motif of rebuilding and starting anew.

The Benilde Committee on Student Involvement functions as the main coordinating body of all duly accredited and recognized Student Organizations in De La Salle-College of Saint Benilde.
